What is the Whisky Order?

A whisky club like no other, The Whisky Order sends you up to 5 unique 30ml tastings of whisk(e)y each month. Each whisk(e)y sample will be hand drawn from a curated selection of Scotch and worldwide whisky. You will be taken on a journey through the most exciting and adventurous whisky on earth.

Purchased separately, the bottles used in our tastings would cost well over $100 EACH due to the premium nature of our selections. But through months of careful negotiations (and a few drams of Scotch), The Whisky Order is able to offer these wonderful malts as a low-cost monthly parcel.
